Housing price tracking

Time series of Zillow home value index tracking for U.S. metropolitan area (historical and future forecasts)

The interactive graph below includes two different plots: a comparison of time series for top 100 US metropolitan area; and time series plot for selected individual location. For the comparison plot, the monthly percentage change with respect to the 2000 Jan level was calculated, along with an estimated index of rate of monthly change. This rate of change is based on monthly, month-to-month percentage changes and applied with a 12-month moving average filter, and thus may be lagging with respect to the monthly price change (e.g., the moving averaged rate of change can be positive while the monthly price already decreases from the price of the previous month). For the plot of individual location, the Zillow home value index and the rate of change are presented. Two dashed lines are additionally plotted, presenting the average month-to-month percent change from 2000 and the zero line.

Zillow data can be downloaded at here.

It may take up to a minute for the graphs to be loaded. View the webpage using desktop browser is recommended.
